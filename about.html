About Page

Hello, and welcome! This website is dedicated to tracking (and maybe even preventing) earthquakes.

The visualization on the home page allows you to interact with real data and see how earthquakes and tectonic plates coincide.

Earthquakes can have a magnitude up to 10 so I added filters for extreme earthquakes (8+) even though they are rare.

So don't be surprised if there is no marker!

Also, negative earthquakes are earthquakes that cannot be felt by humans.

This visualization is easy to use, just use your mouse to explore it!

Findings

Based on the API we can see that...

  1. Earthquakes have evident correlations to the tectonic plates

  2. Certain countries near the plates are at a higher risk for earthquake activity

  3. Thus, countries near the plates should take measures to protect infrastructure
